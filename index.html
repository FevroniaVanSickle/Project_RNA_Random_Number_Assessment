For the next two minutes, you will be asked to complete a task in which you will enter a random number (between 1 and 9) at every one-second interval. You should try to be as random as possible. Picking random numbers can be thought of in terms of picking numbers out of a hat, returning it, shaking the hat’s contents, and then picking another number out of the hat. Doing this may sometimes, although not typically, result in the same number occurring two or more times in a row (e.g., 2, 2, 2). For this task you will be asked to enter a series of random numbers from 1 to 9. Once you press START TASK, you will hear a soft beep (turn volume up) and you will have a box to enter a number from 1 to 9. As soon as you enter one number, another beep will sound and a new box will appear. You will keep doing this task for a couple of minutes. Try and be as random as possible with your number entries. You will have just one second to enter each number, so have your fingers on the number keys and be ready to go before you press start! If you fail to enter a number within 1 second, you will receive a warning, “too slow!” If this happens, just continue to do your best to enter numbers each second. The green bar at the bottom will indicate how much longer you have left in the task.
